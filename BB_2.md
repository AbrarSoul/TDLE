## Criteria

A keyword qualifies when it:

1. Appears in the **correct option text** of **2 or more** questions
2. Does **not** appear in any **incorrect** option
3. Does **not** appear in any **question text**

Matching is case-insensitive substring matching.

For labeled keywords (from explanations), a separate table lists terms that appear more often in correct than incorrect answers, even if they also appear in wrong options.
