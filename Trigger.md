## Reverse-Format Questions (trick wording)

These ask "when is X **not** required?" — the correct answer is that it **is always** required:

| Q# | Question trick | Pick option with |
|---|---|---|
| 82 | When must driver **not** wear seat belt? | **aina käyttää turvavyötä** (B) |
| 140 | When must driver **not** wear seat belt? | **aina käyttää turvavyötä** (A) |
